This project is regarding a fitness application. Which includes many type of exercise and workouts. These exercises and workout are of many types.
Like:- for the growing upper body we have to choose upper body exercise which are to be set in a specific column, as this different parts of body needs different workouts. So my app contains many different types of exercises in it. 
Project functions
This application include many functions which are helpful for our users. Firstly application includes many type of pictures and videos of different fitness trainers. Secondly, users set their fitness goals by weakly and monthly. Thirdly, users track their protein and nutrition intakes. They will make their own exercises bundles by selecting different workouts. They set reminder for their diet and exercises time, so that they can not miss their goals. One more important thing for this time is social media, therefore we set a feature for users to share their progress  on different social media platforms.
